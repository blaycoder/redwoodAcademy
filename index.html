Brief History of Redwood Academy

REDWOOD started in the summer (Raining Season) of August 2014. In September of that year we kicked off the session with 18 teaching and non-teaching staff plus 22 children who occupied all the classes from crèche to primary 6. In other words, we had at least a child in every class.

We have grown over the years in terms of staff strength, number of children, content, facilities and infrastructure. We continue in the spirit of excellence with our vision before us to produce creative, innovative and enthusiastic lifelong learners.

Message from the Executive director

Hello and welcome to our website. Redwood academy was founded in 2014 to provide a comprehensive educational experience that is both rich in content, globally competitive and affordable to residents of Ilamose estate and environs.

We strive to utilise the best blend of learning methodologies which is child-centered, developmentally appropriate and versatile. We believe that children are born ready to learn and with the right environment and personnel, they will actively acquire academic, affective and executive functioning skills, such as, communication, collaboration, critical thinking creativity, decision making, initiative, grit and perseverance. These skills have been termed, “21st century learning skills,” the same skills experts have concluded must be entrenched to be successful in school and in life.
