Our Admission process

  1. Call 08087170346 or visit the school to get all your questions answered
  2. Purchase admission form
  3. Write a placement test
  4. Admission forms are returned with the necessary documents
  5. School fees settlement.
  6. Child is admitted

We cater for the academic, moral, physical and spiritual development of children in our care by employing the Nigerian curriculum with a unique blend of the British and Montessori. Our approach to teaching and learning is one that is deep, active and engaging. We believe children are capable learners and will excel when supported by warm and effective teachers. Our teachers are pofesionals, regularly trained and possess great love for what they do. We offer extra’s such as Music, Ballet, Abacus Mental Math, Coding and Chess plus intervention classes for children having challenges in specific learning areas. Make an appointment now to come see for yourself.

We strive to utilise the best blend of learning methodologies which is child-centered, developmentally appropriate and versatile.
